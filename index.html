Youth To The People Age Prevention Cleanser

1 - Cleanser

Start off with an oil-based cleanser to remove makeup, dirt and oil from skin. Massage it over your dry face, emulsify with lukewarm water, and continue to massage away. Follow up with a water-based gel or foam cleanser to wash away any leftover residue.


Fresh Rose Deep Hydration Toner

2 - Toner

Opt for a hydrating and fortifying toner to refresh your skin’s pH balance and prep your skin to absorb more moisture. Soak up a cotton pad or use your fingertips to gently sweep over your face and neck. Toner helps keep your skin smooth and protected and enables your next products to work more effectively.


SK-II Facial Treatment Essence

3 - Essence

Splash some moisturizing essence into your palm, warm between your hands to activate the ingredients, and gently pat over your entire face and neck. Essences can provide a range of benefits from hydrating and plumping skin to brightening and evening out skin tone.


SK-II Genoptics Spot Essence

4 - Treatment

Personalize your routine with treatments that target your specific skin concerns, such as spot treating acne, dark spots, or fine lines. Apply only as much as you need. Treatments include a potent concentration of ingredients so a few drops can go a long way.


belif The True Cream Aqua Bomb

5 - Moisturizer

Lock in all the moisture and treatments with this final step. Find a moisturizer formulation – emulsion, lotion, gel, cream or oil – that works for your skin type. You could use different ones in the morning and nighttime depending on how your skin is feeling. Hydration is the key to dewy, glowing skin!

Skin Tips and FAQs

  • Is it okay to mix and match brands?
  • Of course! Choose individual products that work best for your skin rather than strictly sticking to one brand.
  • How do I know which product is right for me?
  • Skincare isn't one-size-fits all. Identify your skin type and concerns, and discover which ingredients work best for you via research and trial-and-error.
  • Do I have to do every step?
  • Customize your routine with however many steps, but don't skip cleansing and moisturizing!
  • How often should I follow this routine?
  • AM and PM daily. Once you establish a routine, it will become second nature.
